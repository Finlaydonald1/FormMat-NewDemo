The Problem

Physiotherapy Shouldn't Be This Hard to Access

Millions of people need physical rehabilitation but face barriers that prevent them from getting proper care. The result? Chronic pain, poor mobility, and declining health.

  • Long waiting times — NHS physiotherapy waitlists can exceed 18 weeks, leaving patients in pain without guidance.
  • Expensive private sessions — At £50-100 per session, regular physiotherapy is unaffordable for most people.
  • No feedback at home — Patients doing exercises alone have no way to know if their form is correct.
  • Low adherence rates — Without accountability, most people give up on their rehabilitation programs.
The Solution

Smart Technology for Better Recovery

FormMat combines intelligent hardware with AI coaching to make professional-grade physiotherapy accessible from anywhere.

AI Pose Detection

Our camera system tracks your body position in real-time, analysing joint angles and movement patterns to ensure you're performing exercises correctly.

Pressure Sensor Mat

The smart mat detects weight distribution, balance, and pressure points—providing feedback on stance, posture, and proper load bearing during exercises.

Real-Time Coaching

Receive instant audio and visual feedback as you exercise. FormMat tells you when to adjust, celebrates your progress, and keeps you motivated throughout your session.

How It Works

Your Virtual PT in 3 Simple Steps

1

Set Up Your Space

Roll out the pressure sensor mat and position the camera. The app guides you through a quick 2-minute calibration to learn your body and environment.

2

Follow Your Program

The camera tracks your movements while the mat monitors your balance and weight distribution. Get real-time corrections just like having a physio beside you.

3

Track Your Progress

Review detailed analytics on your form, consistency, and improvement over time. Share reports with your healthcare provider for integrated care.
