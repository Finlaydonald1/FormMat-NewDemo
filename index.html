The problem

We sit too much and move too poorly.

Sedentary lifestyles are quietly eroding our health. Even when we do exercise, poor form and inconsistent routines can lead to pain, injury, and frustration—pushing us back to the sofa.

!

How it works

Camera + pressure mat = smarter, safer movement.

FormMat combines a depth-aware camera with a pressure-sensitive mat to act as a virtual coach in your living room—guiding every rep and every session.

1
Set up your space

Place the FormMat on the floor, position your camera (laptop, TV, or phone) in front of you, and stand on the mat. The system calibrates your height, stance, and range of motion in under a minute.

2
Move with real-time guidance

As you squat, lunge, or press, the camera tracks your joints while the mat senses pressure and balance. FormMat flags unsafe positions, suggests micro-adjustments, and counts clean reps automatically.

3
Get custom workouts that evolve

After each session, FormMat analyses your form, fatigue, and consistency. It then builds tailored workouts—progressing difficulty, targeting weak links, and scheduling short movement breaks to offset sedentary time.
